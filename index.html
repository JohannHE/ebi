About

Welcome to EBITRENDS by Jaco Enslin

Ebitrends is a newsletter and also offers custom research on requested topics. We collect and make sense of online data across multiple channels in order to identify products and services which are becoming more popular among consumers. We then quantify this data and identify which listed companies will stand to benefit from the emerging trends.

Finding cheap/undervalued companies is not particularly hard. The problem is that these companies often remain cheap/undervalued for extended periods of time until a catalyst occurs which unlocks the underlying value in the company. Ebitrends sets out to find these developing catalysts and link them back to their potential impact on undervalued companies’ revenues.

Data is collected from various online sources including Twitter, Reddit, Google, surveys, forums, etc.. People’s thoughts, opinions and preferences are increasingly being posted online in various shapes and forms. Powerful investment insights can be gained by aggregating and making sense of the posts and searches occurring online. As such online data analytics provides important data points to consider when evaluating any consumer goods – or services company.
